Course We Offer

IET offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses across various disciplines of engineering and technology.

B.E.

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) is an undergraduate academic degree awarded to students upon completion of a four-year full-time engineering program. It is a highly sought-after degree that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of various engineering disciplines along with practical skills and hands-on experience.

M.E.

The Master of Engineering (M.E.) program at the Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET), Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV) in Indore is an advanced postgraduate degree program designed for engineering professionals seeking to deepen their expertise in specific areas of specialization. The M.E. program typically spans over two years and offers a range of specialized tracks in various branches of engineering.

M.Sc.

The Master of Science (M.Sc.) program at the Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET), Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV) in Indore is a rigorous postgraduate degree program designed to provide students with advanced theoretical knowledge and practical skills in specialized fields of study within engineering and technology.
